Scope Notes
Included: custom website design and development based on Chelas' outline, ADA-conscious layout approach, local SEO page structure, structured metadata planning, and launch preparation.

Not included: monthly hosting or platform fees, paid apps or plugins, copywriting from scratch, photography or video production, logo or brand redesign, advanced ecommerce beyond gift cards and basic merchandise, or ongoing SEO and blog content creation.

Quantity refers to estimated hours.

Payment 1: 50% on or before project start
Payment 2: 50% on or before project launch

Project Highlights

This estimate covers a larger restaurant build with ADA accessibility, local SEO structure, multi-location content, catering conversion pages, gift cards, merchandise, and policy pages.

Key Priorities
ADA compliance, fast mobile performance, clear ordering paths, strong heading hierarchy, readable contrast, keyboard-friendly interactions, and SEO-rich page architecture for San Clemente and Laguna Beach searches.

Main Pages
Homepage, Locations parent page, 3 location pages, Menu, Catering, About, Gift Cards, Merchandise, Blog / News, Contact, Careers, and Accessibility / Policies.

SEO Focus
Schema markup, internal linking, descriptive metadata, structured local landing pages, performance-minded front-end implementation, and blog structure targeting restaurant, catering, and local intent keywords.

Accessibility Focus
Semantic page structure, heading order, image alt text guidance, contrast-aware styling, tap target sizing, responsive legibility, and an ADA-conscious review pass before launch.
